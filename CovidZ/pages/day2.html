CovidV

A choose your own adventure game by Daniel Sheehan.


The Story So Far

A muted strain of the covid-19 virus has emerged, turning people into the living dead. Because of your
video game addiction, this has happened unknown to you. WHen you finally discover what is going on, you are
not prepared. You mananged to survive your first encounter with a covidz zombie but avoiding any encounter
with a covidz zombie but with supplies non-existent you need to brave the outdoors once more.
Can you survive CovidZ?
