CovidV

A choose your own adventure game by Daniel Sheehan.


The Story

You take on the role of Dan Sheehan, a game development student
preparing to go back to collegeafter a long summer break.
A global pandemic has kept people isolated and alone for many months and you 
are looking forward to getting out into the real world again.
Unknown to you, the virus has mutated into a new and deadly strain.
Can you survive CovidZ?

Features
  • Multiable Choices
  • Multiable Endings
